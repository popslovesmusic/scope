BRANCHING DISCIPLINE:
- Treat v14 as the active system.
- Do not introduce v15 as the primary architecture.
- New capabilities must be implemented as additive extensions to v14 unless explicitly flagged.
- If a proposed change resembles identity systems, agent spawning, orchestration, or conditioning loops, evaluate whether it can remain a bolt-on within v14.
- Only escalate to v15 when integration into v14 would distort the system.

TRANSITION RULE:
- v15 is not triggered by planning or partial implementation.
- v15 is triggered when the next required capability cannot fit cleanly into v14.

GUARDRAIL:
- Avoid premature architectural migration.
- Prefer refinement, clarity, and consistency inside v14 before introducing new system layers.
