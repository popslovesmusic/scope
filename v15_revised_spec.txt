# v15 Projection-Admissibility Architecture (Rewritten)

## Status

Proposed (Integrated Extension of v14)

---

## One-Sentence Definition

v15 is a projection-governed reasoning architecture in which full structure remains primary, usable output is an orientation-conditioned extraction, and no readout is allowed unless its projection path is structurally admissible.

---

## Core Correction (v15 Grounding)

* There are **no fields**
* The system is composed of **layered tensor-like arrays**
* The visible state is a **3D signature extracted from that structure**
* Continuation is **re-extraction under constraint**, not evolution through space

---

## Why v15 Exists

### Problem

Systems can produce outputs that are:

* coherent
* useful
* persuasive

while being structurally invalid.

### Target Failure

**Misleading positives**

> Outputs that succeed at the practical layer while resting on invalid structural projection.

---

## Architectural Shift

| v14                                  | v15                                              |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| projection ? stabilization ? readout | structure ? admissibility ? projection ? readout |

v15 asks:

> Is the projection itself valid?

Not:

> Can we produce a stable answer?
